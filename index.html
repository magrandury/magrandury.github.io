My Story

How I became a Data Scientist

  • 2011

    I officially became a geek

    I was selected for a 4-years program of the Spanish Real Academy of Science that stimulates the mathematical talents of young students, ESTALMAT.

  • 2015

    I started my Maths studies

    I graduated High School as an honor student and started a Double Degree in Mathematics and Physics at the University of Oviedo.

  • 2018

    I was really into Stats

    It was my 3rd year learning statistics and I got an internship as Data Analyst at SADEI, the company responsible for compiling statistics of the Principality of Asturias.

  • 2019

    I broke into AI

    I took the DeepLearning.AI DL Specialization as a recommendation from a teacher I had during my exchange year in Paris and got an internship as Software Engineer at Stelliant, where I developed my first chatbot.

  • 2020

    I'm having a crazy year

    I wrote my 2 bachelor thesis: I researched the algebraic algorithms behind DL and applied DL to study a physics problem. Apparently I had too much spare time so I learned SQL and Tableau and passed the TensorFlow Developer Certification.
