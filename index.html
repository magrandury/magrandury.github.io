My Story

I wrote my first line of code when I was 10, I'm still programming and I always will!

  • 2011

    I officially became a geek

    I was selected for a 4-years program of the Spanish Real Academy of Science that stimulates the mathematical talent of
    young students, ESTALMAT.

  • 2015

    I started my Math studies

    I graduated High School as an honor student and started a Double Degree in Mathematics and Physics at the University of Oviedo.

  • 2018

    I was really into Stats

    It was my 3rd year learning statistics and
    I got an internship as a Data Analyst at SADEI, the company responsible for compiling the statistics of the north of Spain.

  • 2019

    I broke into AI

    I took the DeepLearning.AI DL Specialization as a recommendation from a teacher I had during my exchange year in Paris. I got an internship as a Software Engineer at Stelliant, where I developed my first chatbot.

  • 2020

    It was a crazy year

    I wrote my 2 theses and graduated!
    I developed a chatbot for REDYTEL, another that answers questions about COVID-19, learned how to use Tableau, and passed the TensorFlow Developer Certification. At the end of the year I started contributing to Hugging Face and was selected to join Nova Talent!

  • 2021

    I'm excited about
    my new life

    I've relocated to Berlin and joined neurocat as a Research Engineer! I'm also a core member of the Content Team of Women in AI and I volunteer for #VizForSocialGood.
